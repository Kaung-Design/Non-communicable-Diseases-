The purpose our organization:
Nowadays, the causes of non-communicable diseases are really serious and the death rate of non-communicable diseases are increasing year by year. Read more in our website and you can save yourself and the people around you from non-communicable diseases by donating and volunteering. The purpose of our organization is to help the people with non-communicable diseases and to educate the community about non-communicable diseases to be aware of them. The another purpose is to help the people who want to participate in volunteering in non-communicable diseases programs.






MISSION STATEMENT

Health Guardian(HG) improves the health of the public by strengthening states and expertise for non-communicable diseases prevention and control. HG promotes well being so that communities can built healthier futures.
