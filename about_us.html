About us

Health Guardian is an organization dedicated to improving global health by addressing the growing threat of non-communicable diseases (NCDs). NCDs, such as heart disease, stroke, cancer, diabetes, and chronic lung diseases, are the leading cause of death worldwide.

Health Guardian organization founders, Mr. Kaung Thant Zaw, Mr. Zwin Hte Wah @ Daniel, Mr. Min Thuta Kyaw and Mr. Oak Soe Han, are the Year 7 students of EDUSN digital school, Myanmar.

Our organization started in 10.11.2024. Our organization came from 5 countries:

  • Myanmar
  • The USA
  • The UK
  • South Africa
  • North Africa
  • Singapore




Health Guardian Response

We are committed to preventing and managing these diseases through our goals:

We will support policies and programs that promote healthy lifestyles and reduce the risk factors for NCDs. We will educate individuals and communities about the causes, symptoms, and prevention of NCDs. We support research to develop new treatments and prevention strategies for NCDs. We will partner with individuals, organization, school to prevent NCD and educate them.


Our Mission: To educate individuals and communities to live healthier lives and reduce the burden of NCDs.




Our Goals

Support policies that promote healthy lifestyles and reduce risk factors for NCDs.



Educate communities on causes, symptoms, and prevention of NCDs.



Support research to develop treatments and prevention strategies for NCDs.



Partner with individuals, organizations, and schools for education and prevention.
