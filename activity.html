Activity

'Health Guardians' helps the people with non-communicable diseases. We also share the pamphlets about non-communicable diseases to all ages to educate them. We give them the knowledge not to do the precipitating factors of non-communicable diseases.
Our Mission: To empower individuals and communities to live healthier lives and reduce the burden of NCDs.


For the past weeks we have been focusing on doing activities about NCD for example like interviewing, making posters and writing blog.


Recent Activities

We've been actively working on raising awareness about NCDs and encouraging healthy living. Here are some of our recent efforts:

  • Conducted interviews with healthcare professionals.
  • Created and distributed informative posters.
  • Produced a video series on the impact of NCDs.
  • Published a blog on the importance of lifestyle changes.





























We interviewed to the people who have non-communicable diseases to know the basic knowledge of them. We also educated them to be aware of their non-communicable diseases.
